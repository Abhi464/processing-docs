Sound

Sound was developed to provide simple means for creating sound and interact with processing. The functionality provided entails playing, analyzing and synthesizing sound. Sound comes with a collection of oscillators for basic wave forms. A variety of noise generators like pink, white or brown noise are available. Effects like delay, reverb or filters to alter soundfiles or other generated sound are provided. The syntax is kept simple so patching one soundObject into another is very easy. To visualize sound you can use the analyzer classes Amplitude and FFT.
