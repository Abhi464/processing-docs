Sound

The Sound library was developed to provide simple means for creating sound and interconnect it to other classes and functions of processing.The functionality provided entails playing, analyzing and synthesizing sound. Sound comes with a collection of the basic oscillators. A variety of noise generators like pink, white or brown noise are available. The effects section provides classes like delay, reverb or filters to alter soundfiles or other generated sound. It is easy to patch one Soundobject into the analyzers (volume, FFT) or the effects.
